Our Story

Maine btaya tha n wo book likh rahi hai mere uppar pata nahi wo bhi kabhi mujhe milegi complete hoke ki nahi. usne aur bhi cheeze likhi hai aur ek baar jab uski bahut tabiyat kharab thi mtlb serious tha ekdum to us time usne mujhe ek cheez bheji thi jaane se pehle itna rona aa rha tha dekh ke usko. How they desired to stay together but destiny created another game of its own The transparent wall in a middle us was like someone is stabbing in my heart, His hands was over mine but still I couldn't feel them, While staring at his pale face my tears spilt over and flowed down my cheeks like a river escaping a dam. With my blurry eyes, I wanted to see every single feature of him. And then he asked me to close my eyes in his crumbling voice, Being aware that he won't be there in blink of my eyes, but How can I deny to his last only wish? "Please" he murmured But how? The core of my body ache in a thought of losing my only love. Between the state of my utter chaos, suddenly I heard him saying "I love you so much" All I felt was like someone took all the grief from me. Deep in thought I wonder that whether it is a dream of mine and I close my eyes to see him with my heart. Being aware with the result that when I opened my eyes he was gone from my sight. That wall was still the same, the only difference was that he was no longer standing behind it. I wish he would have taken a moment and stayed for a while a sharp pain surged through and my eyes brimmed with tears. Ye likhi thi tab aur five feet apart ka wo last scene tha 🥺 aur bhi likhti hai mil nahi rahe ek ye likhi thi haal hi me The pale crescent moon shone like a silvery claw in the night sky. We were sitting in the balcony of my house, looking up at the blanket of stars that stretched to infinity, Being wrapped in his arms with a cosy muffled breath on my neck was enough to make me feel like home. Then he gently pulled his leg out, While holding my hand, he grabbed my waist to make me stand. My mind in uncertainty was unable to react, Uncontrollable feelings surged through my body, and my fingers tingles in delight. He smiled then grabbed my hand and turned me around, That one turn and my eyes closed on their own. As if i took a round of the sun at once and all the stars in the sky were applauding to our dance. All of a sudden he drew me closer to himself and leaned his lips on my head We continued to dance, too lost in the music to halt. My body was acting on its own, no chains to hold me back from this pure paradise.

Aur ab to kya hi bolu ye abhi kuch nahi hai itni sari cheeze hai par ye itna sa hi bahut ho gya hai.
