How all of these started

Online mili wo mujhe, ab tak bhi mai usse mila nahi kabhi jab milna bhi hota to kuch n kuch ho hi jata. Bahut log bahut kuch sochenge ye sab dekh ke par koi nahi wo mai hi janta hu kya hai kya nahi aur wo sab kya tha kya hai pata nahi par jo bhi hai aur tha sab sach hai real hai dil se hai.
